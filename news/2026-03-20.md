The philosophical king of Silicon Valley just declared the era over in four words. And judging by what's happening across the ecosystem this week, he might be right.

### 🔥 The Big Stories

**Naval Declares the Software Era Dead**

[Naval's tweet](https://x.com/naval/status/2032893617644384525) "Software was eaten by AI" hit 48K+ engagement for a reason — it crystallizes what every founder is feeling right now. We're past the point of AI-as-feature. We're watching AI consume entire software categories, from code editors to customer support to content creation. The question isn't whether your software company will be disrupted by AI anymore. It's whether you're the one doing the disrupting.

**OpenAI's GPT-5.4 API Sees "Great First Week"**

Sam Altman [confirmed](https://x.com/sama/status/2033659889843913000) that GPT-5.4's API launch momentum is building fast, with "builders building fast." Meanwhile, he [reassured users](https://x.com/sama/status/2033960476972093566) that OpenAI isn't shutting down BoneGPT (whatever that is), saying "I think you will love what the team is building." The speed of API adoption suggests developers aren't just kicking the tires — they're shipping production features.

**Jensen Huang's Token-Based Compensation Vision**

NVIDIA's CEO floated a wild idea this week: engineers should get AI token budgets on top of their salaries. As one [retweet noted](https://x.com/ericosiu/status/2034845494233714700), Huang said "If that $500,000 engineer did not consume at least $250,000 worth of tokens, I am going to be deeply alarmed." This isn't just about productivity — it's about fundamentally reimagining how knowledge work gets compensated in an AI-first world.

**Marketplace AI Disruption Coming** — Andrew Chen [predicts](https://x.com/andrewchen/status/2034728848492036329) that marketplace startups are "destined to be massively reinvented by AI," with the strong form being AI taking over entire supply sides of marketplaces.

**Super Micro Computer Scandal** — [Reports emerged](https://x.com/WOLF_Financial/status/2034782304435151303) that SMCI's co-founder was charged with smuggling $2.5B worth of NVIDIA AI chips to China since 2024, including $510M in a single month window.

**The Internet Wasn't Built for Agents** — a16z [highlighted](https://x.com/a16z/status/2034736824527331746) the massive opportunity for startups to create AI agent proxies, noting "if someone would give me a scoped Gmail, I'd adopt it today."
